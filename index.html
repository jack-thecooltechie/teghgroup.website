About Us

Welcome to Tegh Group, The Industry's Premier One-Stop Solution for Electrical and Mechanical Products and Services. With over Four Decades of experience, we have established ourselves as one of the largest channel partners for POLYCAB CABLES, WIRES & OTHER FMEG products across India.
